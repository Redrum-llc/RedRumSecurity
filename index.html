Security Services

We tailor security solutions to match the risk level, environment, and priorities of each client.

Event Security

Security officers for concerts, clubs, private events, sporting events, and public gatherings. We provide access control, crowd management, perimeter monitoring, and incident response.

Corporate & Facility Security

Protection for office buildings, warehouses, retail locations, and commercial properties. We manage entry points, patrols, visitor access, and incident reporting.

Personal / VIP Protection

Close-protection and personal security services for individuals requiring heightened safety. Discreet, professional, and adaptable to changing conditions.

Mobile Patrol Services

Marked patrols for parking lots, neighborhoods, and commercial properties. Ideal for after-hours deterrence and rapid on-site presence.

High-Risk Security

Security for locations and events in higher-risk areas or with a history of incidents. Strong presence, clear communication, and disciplined response.

Access Control & Monitoring

Control of building entry, guest lists, checkpoints, and restricted areas, supported by clear documentation and reporting.

Mobile Mechanic & Auto Services

In addition to security, we provide honest, practical mechanic services for everyday drivers and small fleets in the Jackson metro area.
